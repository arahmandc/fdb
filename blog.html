Field Data Collection

Bangladesh Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Operational Team (BHOOT) is the core community group of OpenStreetMap Bangladesh. Contributing field level data is a regular basis work of BHOOT by which we produce not only quality data we made mappers to create a better world. For the initiated the project of Humanitarian Map data mappers of Bangladesh Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Operational Team (BHOOT) keep their footprints all places of Mohammadpur and Adabor area to make a difference on the OpenStreetMap. Mapathon For Heath Facilities

Bangladesh Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Operational Team (BHOOT) have arranged a Mapathon for the project of “Make Health Facilities Easear for All” to trace out the health facilities of Dhaka.Most of the contributors of Bangladesh Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Operational Team (BHOOT) have joined in Mapathon. It is a joyous moment of the team to celebrate their mapping party. In the Mapathon various tags of health facilities are discussed among the team members to ensure proper key and value of OSM data.Some moments have captured during the event.
